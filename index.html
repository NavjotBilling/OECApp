About the Competition

The competitions for Ontario Engineering Competition 2014 are Consulting Engineering, Engineering Communications, Innovative Design, Junior & Senior Design, Parliamentary Debate and Programming Competition

<

UOIT is home to the Automotive Center of Excellence and boast one of the only automotive engineering programs in Ontario. Because of this, the 2014 OEC will be challenging participants to improve, innovate and revolutionize the way we look at the automotive industry. Participants will face challenges such as fuel efficiency, onboard computer systems and future policies addressing carbon emissions and vehicle safety.
